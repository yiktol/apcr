AWS Certified Machine Learning Engineer - Associate

The AWS Certified Machine Learning Engineer - Associate (MLA-C01) exam validates a candidate's ability to build, operationalize, deploy, and maintain machine learning (ML) solutions and pipelines by using the AWS Cloud.


Applicable for professionals looking to:

  • Build Skills in Machine Learning: The ideal candidate for this exam has at least 1 year of experience using Amazon SageMaker and other AWS Services for ML engineering.
  • Career Advancement: Advance your career by validating knowledge of ML on AWS

Program Overview

Welcome to the AWS Partner Certification Readiness: Machine Learning Engineer - Associate program!


Validate your cloud expertise as an AWS Certified Machine Learning Engineer - Associate. Earning this certification shows you have the skills to design automated data-based solutions natively in AWS.


This program includes self-paced digital training in AWS Skill Builder and weekly live sessions with our training architects. These sessions provide exam strategy insights, real-world use cases, and hands-on demos of AWS services.

Throughout the program, our AWS Subject Matter Experts will help you:

  • Connect what you learn to real-world customer solutions
  • Develop an exam success strategy and apply this knowledge to your own experiences
  • Apply knowledge and test concepts through a series of practice exam questions

Who should participate?

This offering is designed for AWS Partners with:

  • 1 year of hands-on experience with AWS services (recommended)
  • 1+ year of experience in machine learning engineering or a related field (recommended)
  • Time each week to complete 8+ hours of training and attend the scheduled sessions (recordings available for asynchronous learning)

Getting Started

  1. Visit the Schedule section within the Reception page of the event to add the weekly sessions to your calendar.
  2. Set up your AWS Partner Network (APN) account using your business email. Register here
  3. Next, sign into AWS Skill Builder as an AWS Partner using the credentials from step 2. A Learning Plan for this program will be automatically assigned within 24 hours, but you may enroll directly as well: AWS Skill Builder Learning Plan
  4. (Optional) If you have an AWS Skill Builder Subscription, you may enroll in the Enhanced Exam Prep Plan to access AWS Builder Labs, AWS CloudQuest, Practice Exam, and Enhanced Exam Prep resources.

Having problems? See our APN Set-up and Skill Builder Login video guides.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the scheduled sessions be recorded?

Yes, the scheduled sessions will be available on-demand, within the Replay area. Slides for the Kick-off and Content Review sessions are accessible from the Expo Booth as well. You will find these resources by accessing the event space within RingCentral.

What should I do if I receive a 403 error when accessing courses in AWS Skill Builder?

Make sure that you choose to log into Skill Builder as an AWS Partner and enter your APN Partner Central account information. Learn how to sign into Skill Builder as a partner by watching this short video.

How do I register for the AWS Machine Learning Engineer - Associate exam?

To register for an exam, create an account or sign in at aws.training/certification, then navigate to Exam Registration and follow the prompts. Still need help? Get step-by-step guidance on how to register for an exam as an AWS Partner.

How much does the AWS Machine Learning Engineer - Associate exam cost?

The AWS Certified Machine Learning Engineer - Associate exam fee is 150 USD.

How do I share my AWS Certification achievement with my employer?

Watch this video tutorial to learn how to provide consent for your employer to view your certifications. Remember - your AWS Certification belongs to you and you can always change this setting if your employer changes.

My AWS Certification account name doesn't match my legal ID. How can I update my profile?

To change your name on your AWS Certification Account, submit a ticket to the AWS Training and Certification Support team. Select Account Assistance as the inquiry type and Name Change as the additional details, and include the requested information in the description. If needed, our Support team will request documentation to process the edit. Once all necessary information is submitted, allow up to two days for the change to be processed.

Who should I contact if I experience technical issues?

Having issues with Skill Builder? Review the AWS Skill Builder support website or contact support with specific details about the challenge you're encountering.

Experiencing issues with APN Partner Central? Contact the APN Support team directly.

What if I have additional questions about AWS Certification?

Browse the AWS Certification FAQs on our website.
